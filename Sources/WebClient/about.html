Buying Internet

Connecting to the internet through BitMesh is just like connecting to any other WiFi channel.

Simply select "BitMesh" from your Wi-Fi list, and pay for a set amount of time online using bitcoin. Once paid for, the connection immediately becomes active and you may browse the net as usual. Any unused bitcoin is refunded as soon as the user disconnects.

Selling Your Connection

Sellers who already have access to internet can sell their connection to people nearby for bitcoin.

Sellers simply download the BitMesh software from http://bitmesh.network, and configure their pricing and usage settings. Once the configurations are ready, the seller is ready to broadcast their own BitMesh WiFi channel for buyers to use, straight from their device.
