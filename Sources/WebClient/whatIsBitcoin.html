What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a digital currency used to pay for a variety of goods and services and is accepted by some businesses in place of paper money. Bitcoins are held inside of a digital wallet, which can be downloaded to any device.

Why does BitMesh use Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is especially useful for metered services such as internet data because small transactions can be made without incurring high transaction fees. It does not require people to give out their personal information or sign any contracts in order to gain access to the internet.
